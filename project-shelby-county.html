Shelby County, Kentucky

When my family left Southern California for Rural Kentucky in 1993 you couldn’t get a taco anywhere. Nobody played baseball. People seemed to speak a different language. Snow fell from the sky. When I was older and living in the city I learned about a man named Wendell Berry. His books changed my mind. Turned out he lived just half an hour north on the river. Although I stayed in the city, my heart returned to the land.
