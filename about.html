Hi, my name’s Ricky and I’m a graphic designer & photographer currently working in the field of blindness and disabilities.

I made my first website in 1999 when I was a teenager. In 2004 I finished a degree in Graphic Design. This eventually led me to the American Printing House for the Blind where I’ve worked since 2010, first as a web developer and now as a photographer, videographer, and graphic designer.
